Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We focus on two stages: (1) Start-ups between proof of concept and first commercialisation (R1M-R5M), and (2) Business expansions with proven revenue and scalable models (R5M-R10M).

Our priority sectors include Sustainable Energy, Water Security, Climate Resilience, Bioeconomy, Health Innovation, High-Tech Industrialisation, ICT & Smart Systems, and Mining & Agriculture technology.

Our due diligence process typically takes 8-12 weeks from initial meeting to investment decision. This includes technical validation, financial stress testing, and governance review.

While we prioritise black-owned and women-led ventures as part of our transformation mandate, we evaluate all investment opportunities based on merit, commercial viability, and alignment with our mandate.

ESG principles are embedded in our investment process from sourcing through exit. We conduct ESG screening, monitor compliance, and measure impact through job creation, SME growth, and financial inclusion metrics.
