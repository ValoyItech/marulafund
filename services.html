Secondary Capability

Full Project Development Lifecycle

Beyond venture capital, we provide advisory services across nine integrated phases of project development—from conception to financial close.

1

Project Conception

Strategic framing, alignment assessment, and early risk identification to test whether a project deserves capital.

2

Pre-Feasibility Studies

Technical screening, cost estimation, and financial viability testing to eliminate non-viable projects efficiently.

3

Full Feasibility

Investment-grade readiness confirmation, technical buildability, and bankability preparation for lender scrutiny.

4

Financial Modelling

Limited recourse project finance models, scenario analysis, and sensitivity testing across all risk variables.

5

Valuation

Discounted cash flow valuation, comparable transaction benchmarking, and fairness opinion analytical support.

6

Deal Structuring

M&A advisory, risk allocation consistency testing, and independent model audit readiness.

7

Project Planning

Development oversight, procurement structuring, and contract negotiation advisory.

8

Financial Close

Transaction support, lender covenant alignment, and reserve account modelling.

9

Implementation

Oversight and handover support, ensuring projects transition successfully to operational phase.
