Full Project Development Lifecycle

Beyond venture capital, we provide advisory services across nine integrated phases—from conception to financial close.

1

Project Conception

Strategic framing, alignment assessment, and early risk identification to test whether a project deserves capital.

2

Pre-Feasibility

Technical screening, cost estimation, and financial viability testing to eliminate non-viable projects efficiently.

3

Full Feasibility

Investment-grade readiness confirmation, technical buildability, and bankability preparation for lender scrutiny.

4

Financial Modelling

Limited recourse project finance models, scenario analysis, and sensitivity testing across all risk variables.

5

Valuation

6

Deal Structuring

7

Project Planning

8

Financial Close

9

Implementation

Oversight and handover support, ensuring projects transition successfully to operational phase.
