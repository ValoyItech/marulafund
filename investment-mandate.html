Our Focus

Technology Impact Venture Capital

Marula Fund Managers is positioned as a first-time innovation-focused VC manager responding directly to South Africa's technology development and commercialisation agenda. We concentrate on ventures between product development or proof of concept and first commercialisation stage.

This is deliberate. The highest value creation and the highest failure risk sit in this window. We bridge the capital gap where failure rates are highest and partner for follow-on capital toward full commercialisation.

Investment Thesis

"We do not fund ideas. We fund validated execution pathways. Every investment must survive technical scrutiny, market realism, financial stress testing, and governance review before capital is deployed."

Selection Criteria

What We Look For

Stage 1: Start-Ups

R1 Million - R5 Million

  • Proof of concept completed
  • Early product validation achieved
  • Technology defensible or IP backed
  • Initial customer traction visible
  • Founder-led management teams
  • Revenue emerging or first contracts secured
  • High technical or commercial risk acceptable
  • Strong alignment with national development priorities

Stage 2: Expansion

R5 Million - R10 Million

  • Revenue generating businesses
  • Proven product-market fit
  • Positive unit economics
  • Established customer base
  • Scalable operational model
  • Regulatory alignment confirmed
  • Management depth beyond founder
  • Clear expansion thesis & defined exit visibility

Capital Deployment

Investment Instruments

We deploy capital through flexible instruments tailored to each venture's stage, risk profile, and growth trajectory.

Equity Instruments

  • Seed equity investments
  • Structured growth equity
  • Performance-linked ratchets
  • Preferred equity structures

Convertible Instruments

  • Convertible notes
  • SAFE aligned instruments
  • Convertible equity
  • Milestone-based conversion

Best for: Early-stage ventures with future valuation uncertainty

Debt Instruments

  • Venture debt
  • Revenue-based financing
  • Equipment financing
  • Working capital facilities

Best for: Revenue-generating ventures with predictable cash flows
