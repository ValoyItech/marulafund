Mutual Confidentiality Undertaking

By submitting your project to Marula Fund Managers, you acknowledge and agree to the following terms:

  1. Confidential Information: All project details, financial data, technical specifications, business plans, and proprietary information disclosed ("Confidential Information") shall be treated as strictly confidential.
  2. Purpose: Confidential Information shall only be used for the purpose of evaluating potential investment, project preparation support, or capital raising assistance.
  3. Non-Disclosure: Marula Fund Managers undertakes not to disclose, reproduce, distribute, or use Confidential Information for any purpose other than evaluation without prior written consent.
  4. Standard of Care: Both parties shall exercise the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as they use to protect their own proprietary information, but in no event less than reasonable care.
  5. Exceptions: This undertaking does not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach; (b) was rightfully in possession prior to disclosure; (c) is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction; or (d) is independently developed without use of Confidential Information.
  6. Term: This obligation shall survive for a period of five (5) years from the date of submission.
  7. Return of Information: Upon request, all physical and electronic copies of Confidential Information shall be returned or destroyed, except for archival copies maintained in standard backup procedures.
  8. Remedies: Both parties acknowledge that breach may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages would be inadequate, and consent to injunctive relief.

Common Questions

Submission FAQ

What happens after I submit my project?
Upon submission, you receive an automatic acknowledgment within 24 hours. Our team conducts an initial screening for sector alignment, stage appropriateness, and documentation completeness within 5 business days. Eligible projects are invited to a preliminary call, following which we provide feedback or proceed to detailed due diligence.
Is my project information secure?
Absolutely. All submissions are covered by our Mutual NDA (linked above). We employ encrypted storage, access controls, and never share project details with third parties without explicit consent. Investor access to pipeline data requires separate NDA execution and subscription verification.
Can I submit multiple projects?
Yes. Each project requires a separate submission to ensure proper tracking and evaluation. However, if submitting related ventures or portfolio companies, please mention this in your submission email or description for coordinated review.
What if my project is early-stage with no revenue?
We accept pre-revenue ventures at proof-of-concept or pilot stage, particularly in priority sectors. The key requirement is demonstrable technical validation, clear market need, and a credible path to commercialisation. Our Stage 1 funding (R1M-R5M) is specifically designed for such ventures.
How do I update my submission with new information?
Reply to your acknowledgment email with updates, or send a follow-up to projects@marulafund.com referencing your original submission date and project name. For significant updates (new financials, term sheets, etc.), we recommend submitting a fresh version with "UPDATE" in the subject line.
